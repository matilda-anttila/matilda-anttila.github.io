Selected profile

I am a cultural sector professional working across theatre, performance, and arts programming. My practice includes writing, directing, choreography, devising, and collaborative production work, alongside a strong interest in accessible audience experiences and cultural work for children and young people.

I am particularly drawn to roles in arts management, production, cultural coordination, communications, and programme support. My experience combines artistic work with project-based collaboration, audience awareness, and a strong understanding of public-facing cultural environments.
